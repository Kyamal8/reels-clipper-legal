Privacy Policy — Reels Clipper

Reels Clipper accesses social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) only after the account owner explicitly connects them via each platform's official OAuth flow.

Data we access

OAuth access/refresh tokens needed to upload video content on the connected account's behalf, and basic public profile information (e.g. channel/account name) used to display which account is connected.

Data storage

Tokens and video files are stored locally on the operator's own server and are not shared with, or sold to, any third party. Data is used solely to publish the videos the account owner submits.

Data deletion

Disconnecting an account in the app immediately deletes its stored tokens. To request full data deletion, contact sadikyar.kyama@gmail.com.
