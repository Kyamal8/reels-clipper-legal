Terms of Service — Reels Clipper

Reels Clipper is a personal video-editing tool that turns long-form videos into short vertical clips and, at the user's explicit request, publishes those clips to the user's own connected social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram).

Use of the service

This app is operated by a single individual for personal use. It only publishes content on behalf of accounts that the account owner has explicitly connected and authorized via OAuth. No content is posted without the account owner's direct action.
